2018 — 2019
Communication Expert at Uabalika: #InspireAmor #EspalheAmor﻿
Mozambique - Dondo, Beira

The #InspireAmor #EspalheAmor campaign was an initiative carried out in the Dondo district, Sofala province, in response to the devastating impact of Cyclone Idai. The project aimed to support families and women affected by the disaster, providing humanitarian assistance and promoting solidarity. Cyclone Idai, considered one of the most intense to hit Mozambique, caused severe flooding, resulting in more than 500 deaths and directly affecting more than 2.5 million people, many of whom were left in a situation of extreme vulnerability.

As part of this campaign, I worked as a communication expert, specifically in the production of audiovisual content, including videography, design and image capture. The material produced played a key role in mobilizing support and raising awareness among humanitarian organizations and donors, helping to amplify the urgent needs of the affected population.

Idai was the tenth named storm and the seventh tropical cyclone of the 2018-2019 southwest Indian cyclone season. Its origin dates back to a tropical depression formed off the east coast of Mozambique on March 4.

The cyclone came and went, leaving traces of destruction and displaced families. Yet, in contact with these families, we realized that there is a positioning and a will to start again since the most important thing that remained in the midst of all this is life.

2021
Madala App at Toptal﻿

*Project developed for Toptal Screening Process November 2021

Assignments: Research | UX Strategy | UX Design

The Brief:

Community connection app for seniors.
Design a tablet dashboard that connects residents that live in the same private community (a building, for example).

The target audience is seniors (60+) who see accessibility and retaining an active social life as a key concern. Some of the key functionalities of the platform are to provide an easy way for residents to connect and communicate with each other, create and stay informed about events, vote on community initiatives, request a repairman, and complete their basic profile.

You may use this as a base set of features, but feel free to add additional features that would take this concept to the next level whilst considering the overall usability of the product.


The Goal

With the situation verified, the first step is to understand our target audience and their experience with mobile devices, which is the means to provide a solution to the problems listed. The goal is to design a platform that guarantees greater flexibility between the target audience (elderly) and the employees or personnel who take care of them, ensuring no situations of complete isolation or depressive problems. The goal includes making it easy to access information about the external world without waiting for the news on T.V. or Radio to entertain them.


The Solution

Create tablet dashboards that connects residents that live in the same private community

The application was designed to work on a tablet, given that the target audience, the age group comprises older people over 60 years of age and, in most cases, they have vision prob- lems, which makes it important that the solution adapts to this reality and makes its usability and reading of the information easy.

Madala App will provide seniors with access to reading groups, running and more activities or hobbies that may interest them. This can be done online or physically, access to up-to-date information about society and health and thus a balanced control of their condition.

Assuming that one of the problems faced by the elderly is a complete overview of the house, the application will have an intuitive map that will help control the course according to the destination in question.
